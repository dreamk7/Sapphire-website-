Meet Our Team

Sarah Johnson

Founder & CEO

Sarah founded Sapphire Charity in 2010 with a vision to create a better future for children worldwide. With over 15 years of experience in nonprofit management, she leads our organization with passion and dedication.

Michael Chen

Program Director

Michael oversees all of our programs and ensures they align with our mission. He brings extensive experience from his work with international NGOs and holds a Master's in Social Work.
